Master Your Craft

The Equipment You Need

Whether your a profesional with alot of equipment or just rocking a phone to take photos. Don't worry as it doesn't matter. We are here to teach the basics and all you need is something that will take a photo!

Settings

We won't get to focused on settings right away otherwise it will become way too boring. Will try to teach them as we go along. Like how you have to balance 'ISO', 'Aperture' and 'Shutter Speed' to get the desired style of photo. So dont sweat it.

Editing

Here comes the fun part. Deciding how to color grade your photos. Think of it as your the artist painting a painting. You dont want to over do it, but not doing it either won't get the results you are looking for. Why edit? Because we shoot in the 'RAW' format which is what the camera sees. But the photos come out in a flat profile for us to manipulate later.

Practice Makes Perfect

If you decide to join this workshop. Firstly great! secondly to see the results, you will need to put the time and effort after in your own time. There is alot to learn. It takes time but as long as your having fun doing it doesn't matter as your doing something you enjoy!

Reasons You Should Join

Develop New Skills

If you decide to join us, the skills you will pick up will last a lifetime to the point where you will always seek to find new perspectives!

Networking

At the time being we are only doing group sessions. But hey, it's not a bad thing. As you will be taking photos and learning with people with the same skill-level. You might even make new long lasting relationships!

You'll have Fun!

The main thing to always remember is to have fun. There's no point doing something if you don't enjoy it. We here at Visual Stories pride ourselves by trying to always keep it interesting. Not only do we just teach photography. After sessions we go hang out grab a coffee or socialise. This let's us have a connection with our students and a better teaching environment.
